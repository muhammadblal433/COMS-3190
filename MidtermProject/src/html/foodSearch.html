Food Search

Enter a food item to get its nutritional information

How To Use It?

  • Type the name of any food item into the search bar (e.g., "Apple," "Pizza," "Chicken Breast").
  • Click the "Get Nutrition Info" button.
  • The page will display detailed nutrition facts, including calories, protein, carbs, and fat.
  • Use this tool to track your food intake and make informed healthy choices.
